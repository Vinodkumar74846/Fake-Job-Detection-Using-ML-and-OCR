To report a fake job in India, you can send an email to the following official authorities:

  • 🛡️ 1. National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal (Government of India)
    📧 Email: cybercrime.gov.in@gov.in (for general contact; reporting is better through the portal)
    🌐 Website to report directly: https://cybercrime.gov.in/
    📌 Category: "Online Job Fraud"
  • 🧾 2. Ministry of Labour and Employment (for labor-related frauds)
    📧 Email: labourcomplaints-mole@gov.in
    🌐 Website: https://labour.gov.in
  • 🏛️ 3. Your Local Police / Cyber Cell
    You can also file an FIR at the nearest police station or contact the cyber crime cell in your city.

Many states also have their own cyber helplines (e.g., 1930 for cyber fraud reporting in India).
